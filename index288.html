About me

My name is Maddy M. Renggli.

I always wanted to be behind the camera and put people, architecture and nature in a good lighting. But I only started taking photographs recently and discovered my love for photography.

I started photography last winter. In module 265 we were asked to create a photography portfolio. In this project I took pictures of my sister, my brother and my boyfriend. I loved doing this and now I am motivated to take photographs more often.
